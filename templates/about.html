About CardioScan AI

CardioScan AI is an advanced heart disease prediction system that leverages deep learning technology to assess cardiovascular health risks based on multiple health parameters.

Our Technology

Our system uses a sophisticated neural network trained on thousands of patient records to identify patterns and risk factors associated with heart disease. The model analyzes 20 different health parameters to generate accurate risk assessments.

Parameters Analyzed

  • Demographic information (age, sex)
  • Lifestyle factors (smoking, activity levels)
  • Medical history (diabetes, hypertension, family history)
  • Vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate)
  • Lab results (cholesterol levels)
  • ECG patterns and cardiac indicators
